Artist Statement

“Creativity is a view into one’s soul”


I feel my artwork is expressive; it helps me express my emotions. If you see the progress of my artwork you would see the different stages in my life. My artwork is about trying to tell a story and expressing that story with emotion. I translate my dreams and experiences through my artwork. My inspiration not only comes from my personal experiences but also from nature, music and books. I have expressed this through different media; Gouache is my favorite. I also have experience in Digital Illustration, Acrylic, Watercolor, Oil, Graphite, Charcoal, Mixed Paper Collage, Ceramics, Metalsmithing and Printmaking.

Throughout the years, I have shown in over 20 exhibitions. My education includes a 4 ­ year Art Emphasis in High School, a Bachelor in Fine Art, with an emphasis in Watercolor, from Western Michigan University and an Associate of Applied Science in Graphic Design and Illustration from Kalamazoo Valley Community College.
